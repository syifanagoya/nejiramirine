The Repair Shop's Angelina Bakalarou's zen den is a plant haven

Angelina is a paper pro who works alongside the rest of The Repair Shop team

    Paper conservator, Angelina Bakalarou, has been delighting visitors to The Repair Shop with her amazing skills, and the viewers love her too! Even after her very first appearance on the show, fans took to social media to welcome the pro. "Welcome to the best television series ever shown on any station!" wrote one and: "Another great talent added to an amazing team," commented another.

    The creative lives in London and the UK capital features heavily on her Instagram feed, but as well as cityscapes and photos of her own personal projects, she's also given her following multiple looks inside her sanctuary-like home that's filled with many plants.
