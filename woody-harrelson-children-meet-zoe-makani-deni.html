Woody Harrelson children: Meet Zoe, Makani, Deni

Woody Harrelson is an American actor and playwright. He is the recipient of various accolades, including a Primetime Emmy Award and two Screen Actors Guild Awards, in addition to nominations for three Academy Awards and four Golden Globe Awards.

Woody Harrelson movies

Harrelson has appeared in several movies which has won him multiple awards, notable films include White Men Can’t Jump (1992), Natural Born Killers (1994), Kingpin (1996), The Thin Red Line (1998), No Country for Old Men (2007), Zombieland (2009), Rampart (2011), Seven Psychopaths (2012), Now You See Me (2013), The Edge of Seventeen (2016), LBJ (2016), Solo: A Star Wars Story (2018), and Triangle of Sadness (2022). He also gained prominence for his portrayal of Haymitch Abernathy in The Hunger Games film series (2012-2015).

Woody Harrelson age

He is 61 years old. He was born on July 23, 1961.

Woody Harrelson biography

Tracy Woodrow Harrelson was born on July 23, 1961, in Midland, Texas, to hitman Charles Voyde Harrelson and clerk Diane. Along with his two brothers, Jordan and Brett, who both became actors, he was brought up in a Calvinist home.

For the murder of federal judge John H. Wood Jr. in 1979, their father was given a life term. According to Harrelson, his father wasn’t always present when he was a kid. On March 15, 2007, Charles passed away at the Administrative Maximum Facility of the United States Prison.

The income of Harrelson’s mother was crucial to the survival of his family. In 1973, he relocated to Lebanon, Ohio, the hometown of his mother, where he enrolled and eventually finished Lebanon High School in 1979. He worked at Kings Island during the 1979 summer season.

Harrelson studied English and drama at Hanover College in Hanover, Indiana. He made acquaintances with Mike Pence, the future vice president, while a member of the Sigma Chi fraternity there. His graduation was in 1983.

Woody Harrelson children

He has three children. They are Zoe Giordano Harrelson, Makani Ravello Harrelson, and Deni Montana Harrelson.
