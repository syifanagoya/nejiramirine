Financial Contributions and Earnings from Acting and Stunt Work

Patricia Tallman has amassed her wealth through various avenues. Her acting roles in major films and television series, combined with her work as a stunt performer, have significantly boosted her income.

Roles in Major Film Productions

Tallman is best known for her role in the 1990 remake of Night of the Living Dead, directed by Tom Savini. She also worked closely with George A. Romero appeared in several films, including Knightriders, Monkey Shines, and Creepshow 2.

Leadership and Earnings at Studio JMS

In 2012, she became the CEO and executive producer of Studio JMS, in partnership with her then-boyfriend, J. Michael Straczynski, the creator of Babylon 5.

Financial Impact of Voice Work and Other Ventures

These projects have added to her diverse portfolio and contributed to her financial stability.
