Watch Paris Saint-Germain stars Neymar, Cavani and Verratti join flight crew in embarrassing advert

Footballers and acting generally don’t go well together.

When David Beckham made his Hollywood debut in Guy Ritchie’s film, ‘King Arthur: Legend of the Stone’, critics tore into his performance.

However, that hasn’t stopped Paris Saint-Germain from trying their hand at it!

In partnership with Emirates Airline, the French champions have created a new advert featuring several star players as members of a cabin crew.

The video sees Neymar and Cavani alongside Adrien Rabiot, Marco Verratti and Thiago Silva wearing pilot outfits and enjoy a kickabout on board ‘Emirates flight number 4-3-3’.

The ‘captain’ talks over the clip, saying: “Ladies and gentlemen, this is your captain speaking.

“Welcome to today's Emirates flight number 4-3-3.

“Joining me today we have an international crew of eight different nationalities that will entertain you for 90 minutes.

"Our entertainment programme offers renowned shows from over the world.

"In a few moments, we'll be ready for the kick-off.

"And remember, in the unlikely event of some turbulence during the match, please start chanting: "Ici c'est Paris!"

While PSG might be soaring high domestically, they came crashing back to the ground during their Champions League game against Real Madrid when they were beaten 3-1.
