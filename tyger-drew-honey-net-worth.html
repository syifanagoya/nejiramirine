What is Tyger Drew-Honey's Net Worth?

Tyger Drew-Honey is an English actor, musician, and television presenter who has a net worth of $2 million. Tyger Drew-Honey was born in Epsom, Surrey, England in January 1996. He is best known for playing the role of Jake in the television series Outnumbered. Drew-Honey starred as Lester Large in the TV series The Large Family in 2010. From 2007 to 2010 he had a recurring role in the series The Armstrong and Miller Show. In 2012 he starred as Mr. Lovett in the television series The Ministry of Curious Stuff.

His best known role came starring as Jake in the series Outnumbered from 2007 to 2014. From 2012 to 2014 Drew-Honey starred as Dylan in the TV series Cuckoo. He starred in the movies Horrid Henry: The Movie in 2011 and Up All Night in 2015. Drew-Honey presented the television series Friday Download from 2011 to 2012. He was nominated for a British Comedy Award for Best Male Comedy Newcomer in 2009 for Outnumbered.
