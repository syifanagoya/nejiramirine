What is Gabriele Corcos' Net Worth?

Gabriele Corcos is an Italian celebrity cook, entrepreneur, and television personality who has a net worth of $5 million. That is a combined net worth with his wife, actress Debi Mazar. Gabriele Corcos was born in Fiesole, Italy in October 1972. He is best known for creating, hosting, and producing the television series Extra Virgin on the Cooking Channel. Gabriele Corcos authored the New York Times best selling cookbook titled Extra Virgin.

He formerly owned The Tuscan Gun Officine Alimentari located in Windsor Terrace, Brooklyn, New York. His wife, actress Debi Mazar, also starred on his television series Extra Virgin Americana. Gabriele and Debi have been married since 2002. The show Extra Virgin was on the air from 2011 to 2015 and Extra Virgin Americana aired in 2016. Corcos has also appeared in episodes of the TV series Hell's Kitchen, Iron Chef America: The Series, Unique Eats, The Talk, The Chew, Chopped, The Wendy Williams Show, Rachael Ray, The Dr. Oz Show, The Kitchen, and more.
