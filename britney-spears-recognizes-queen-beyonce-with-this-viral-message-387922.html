Britney Spears Recognizes Queen Beyonc With This Viral Message

It is a queen being inspired by another queen. On Tuesday, pop star Britney Spears took to Instagram, where she penned an uplifting message and channeling her inner Beyoncé while at it.

Britney, who is in self-quarantine due to the coronavirus, encouraged her fans to practice self-love and to grow during this tough period.

The diva concluded her uplifting message by giving a special shout out to Beyoncé, who once told everyone that they are all flawless.

If you don’t believe me, just look at what God is demonstrating for us to do each day for ourselves ….. self-care and knowing our worth as individuals!!!!!!!. Learning to love You sometimes is the hardest thing to do … and do you know how I show that 🤔🤔🤔 ???? By posting whatever the heck I want 😂😂😂💋💋💋 !!!
