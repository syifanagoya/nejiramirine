Did Erik Conover And Hannah Coleman Split?

Erik Conover and Hannah Coleman have completely erased their relationship from the internet.

Coleman joined Nest Seekers as its head of digital marketing and strategy. The two weren't only surviving; they were thriving.

Then 2021 rolled around, and things took a drastic shift. Coleman deleted every instance of Conover from her social media, and Conover did the same. 

A huge chunk of 2020 has been excised from their social media accounts. Though the two have been very quiet about their breakup, reading between the lines shows that things are very wrong.

Coleman was the first to speak out, albeit in tongues when she released two videos on her channel. The first video, titled "life update," was released on February 16, 2021, and the second video, titled "changing my life... what I've been up to," was released on April 17, 2021.

Things like putting yourself first, not letting anyone dictate your life, and believing in yourself. As Coleman posted the videos soon after her breakup, many people understood that she was referring to her relationship with Conover.

Conover did not respond in a video but did post comments here and there stating that Coleman had broken his heart and that he would not want to marry for a long time.
