Missing Allyana Hernandez Found Safe

The Gonzales Police Department reported that Allyanna Hernandez was discovered safe in Tarrant County, ending the Amber Alert for her.

Allyanna Hernandez, 12, is missing, and the Gonzales Police Department needs your help locating her.

She may be in imminent or serious danger, according to police. Since Saturday at 8 a.m., Allyanna has been gone. In Gonzales, Texas, she was last seen in the 1600-block of St. Vincent Street. About 75 miles east of San Antonio is Gonzales.

Allyanna has a height of five feet and a weight of 90 pounds. She has brown eyes and blonde tresses. She wore spectacles, a white blouse, bell-bottom blue jeans, and white shoes when she was last seen.

Call the Gonzales Police Department at (830) 672-8686 if you have any knowledge.
